Restructure the Lynx Racing site into a real multi-page app instead of a single page.

TECH STACK (additions to v1)
- React Router v6 for routing
- Code-split each route (React.lazy + Suspense) so the 3D viewer bundle only loads on
  the pages that need it

NAV BAR (persistent, all pages)
- Logo + wordmark, left
- Links: Home, The Bike, Sponsors, Events, Team, Join, Contact
- "Partner With Us" button, right, links to /sponsors

ROUTES

/  (Home — hero only, nothing else competes with it)
- Full-bleed section, near-black background (~#05070d).
- 3D canvas centered/full-bleed behind or beside the text: the Lynx bike, slowly
  auto-rotating, continuously, with NO drag/orbit/zoom interaction here — it's decorative,
  like Redback's looping hero video, not a tool. Disable pointer/orbit handlers entirely
  on this page.
- Overlay text: small uppercase eyebrow ("UNSW · Student-Built · Electric Racing"), a large
  heading ("Lynx Racing"), a one-sentence mission line, and a single CTA button
  ("Explore the Bike" -> /the-bike).
- A small stat row under or near the hero — Voltage / Status / Mode — same live values as
  before. Keep these honest to the team's actual current stage (development/testing), the
  way Redback shows real competition results — don't invent race results Lynx hasn't
  earned yet.
- Below the hero, three link-out cards, mirroring Redback's "Join Our Team / Support Us /
  Who Are We" pattern:
  - "The Bike" -> /the-bike — see the full engineering breakdown
  - "Partner With Us" -> /sponsors — sponsorship pitch
  - "Join the Team" -> /join — recruitment
  Each card: one photo/graphic, a short heading, one sentence of body copy, one link.
- A sponsor logo strip: "Our Sponsors" heading, a row of logos (grayscale, brighten on
  hover), "View All" link to /sponsors. Use placeholder logo marks until real ones exist.
- A mailing list signup strip: first name / last name / email / submit, same pattern as
  Redback's footer signup.
- Footer: social icons, copyright line.

/the-bike  (everything from the v1 spec lives here now)
- The subsystem tab bar: Full Bike, Battery, Motor/Drive, Chassis, Suspension,
  Aero/Bodywork, Telemetry.
- Left info panel (module status, heading/description per tab, tag pills, sparkline).
- The full interactive 3D viewer: orbit + zoom enabled, camera presets per tab, subsystem
  highlighting/dimming — exactly as specced in v1, including subsystemMap.json and the
  procedural-placeholder fallback if lynx-bike.glb is missing.
- Bottom live stats bar (Voltage/Status/Mode/System).

/sponsors, /events, /team, /join, /contact
- Simple content pages for now, same dark/neon design tokens as the rest of the site.
- /sponsors: grid of sponsor tiers/logos, contact CTA.
- /team: grid of member cards (photo, name, role).
- /join: what departments exist, how/when recruitment opens, CTA to apply.
- /contact: short form (name, email, message) + socials.
- Not the focus of this pass — structure and style them consistently, keep content minimal
  and easy to expand later.

DESIGN CARRYOVERS FROM REDBACK (reinterpreted in Lynx's palette, not copied verbatim)
- Confident, short stat callouts near the hero rather than paragraphs of self-description.
- One clear photo/graphic + one sentence + one link per card — resist the urge to add more
  per card.
- A single unmistakable CTA per section, never two competing ones.
- Mailing-list capture as a first-class homepage element, not buried in the footer only.
